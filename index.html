About

I am a Computer Science graduate with a Master’s degree and professional experience as a Data Engineer, working at the intersection of large-scale data systems, machine learning, and behavioral analytics. My interests lie in applying computational and statistical methods to study human behavior, mental health, and cognitive processes using real-world data.

I have worked on both industry-scale data pipelines and research-driven machine learning projects, including wearable activity analysis and depression modeling. I am currently preparing for PhD training in computational psychology, cognitive science, and behavioral data science.
